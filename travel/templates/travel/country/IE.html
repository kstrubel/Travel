{{ country_code }} - Ireland

January 2018. My last trip in January before starting school in Switzerland, I visited Barbara in Ireland. She was on exchange at UCD. I did the obligatory tour of the Guinness factory and Book of Kells. Barbara introduced me to Sandmean tours, a service that provides free tours of cities all around the world. I learned a good bit of Irish history and lingo. They like to say "What's the crack" to ask what's going on. I went to a local barbershop and was amazed how different English sounded coming from an Irish fellow. If I didn't know better I'd swear they were speaking a different language. The live music scene was incredible in Dublin as well as Galway across the island. The grass was lucious and green and the Cliffs of Moore impressive in scale.
