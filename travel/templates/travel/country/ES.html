Spring Break 2018.

We met our good friend Carla through classes at ETH. She is from a small beach town in Spain outside Girona called L'Escala. Her family runs a restaurant and they were kind enough to host us in their home for the tail end of Spring Break. Tourist season hadn't started up yet, so we had a couple leisurely days to hang around the sleepy beach town before checking our Girona and ending in Barcelona. We sky dived over the coastal town of Empuriabrava, jumping at 4000 meters. We free fell for a full minute before deploying our chutes at 1500 meters for a total of a five minute flight. After landing, my instructor told me he was surprised how well it went because its difficult jumping with people taller than you... I'm glad he waited to tell me. He told me he'd jumped thousands of times, so to him it was routine.

In barcelona we looked at the beautiful architecture and went to the incredible clubs.

Later in the semester we made a trip out to Valencia to meet Matt's extended family then stay with Purdue students in Madrid.
