{{ country_code }} - France

January 2018.



We flew into Paris from Morocco. We were incredibly excited to eat food our stomachs were more accustomed too. I had eaten some pretty sketchy meals in Morocco and was excited to stuff my face with crepes and croissants. Surprisingly, the highest rated restaurants in the area we were staying were all asian restaurants. My favorite was Kintaro. The line was always down the block. The Paris brasseries always had cool atmosphere to sit outside at night, sipping cognac.

I enjoyed exploring Montmarte and tasting the crepes served out of the cafe windows on the street.

We got a walking tour in broken english of a collection of street art. Not a topic I had much exposure to but thoroughly enjoyed.


You can always count on the corny Rick Steves to reliably guide you through the city. We used his recordings to explore much of the old city.


The catacombs are always an overwhelming sensory experience. It's hard to comprehend that each bone belonged to a human years ago.
