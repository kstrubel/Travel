Noblesville, Indiana
I was born and raised in Noblesville, a suburb of Indianapolis.

West Lafayette, Indiana
Attended Purdue to study Computer Engineering.
Grey House Coffee and Harry's Chocolate Shop are definitely establishments at Purdue.

Bristol, CT
Spent a summer living in the suburbs of Hartford, CT working for GE Electric. Head over to West Hartford for some ritzy restaurants. In Hartford, Black Eyed Sally's is a favorite for it's live jazz music. There's a lot of great parks for hikes or mountain biking throughout the suburbs.

San Jose, CA
Spent a summer living in the bay area working for Cisco Systems.
Plenty of fun things to do in the bay from hiking Mount Diablo to wandering Stanford's beautiful campus. I think Palo Alto has a great variety of food, but nothing beats grabbing a burrito in Mission District in San Fran. If the weather's nice, head over to Dolores Park for an eclectic crowd.

Chicago, IL
Spent a summer living in Chicago working for Deloitte consulting. Giordono's pizza, sausage or spinach with banana peppers, began an instant favorite.
Best places to get drinks in the summer:
-Cindy's - great view of millennium park, gets packed so go early and find a spot at the railing
-Gibson's Italia - not cheap, but their rooftop bar on the canal is my favorite place to have a cocktail when the weather's warm
-Slugger's = for a college bar experience, check out the bar with a full batting cage upstairs
