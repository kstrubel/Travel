{{ country_code }} - Portugal

Spring Break.







After London, we flew into Portugal. Unfortunately, I was getting over food poisoning and wasn't very active for this leg of the journey but here's a couple things I remember.

Portugal is super cheap. Everything from housing to food is much cheaper than the rest of Western Europe. We met some ex pats who retired here for the quality of living they could afford on a budget.

We traveled to a palace, Quinta da Regaleira, in Sintra. In 1904, a rich, eccentric man built a beautiful palace on the southern coast of portugal. It's a mix of architectural styles and contains many symbols alluding to secret societies. The place had a maze of secret underground tunnels and a well believed to be used for Masonic Initiation rituals. Oddly beautiful and creepy.
